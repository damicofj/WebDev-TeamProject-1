Frequently Asked Questions

Can I buy tickets at the Park entrance?
Yes! Tickets are available at the Park entrance, and can be purchased online too.
Which shows can I currently watch at the Park?
For the safety and wellbeing of our guests, all shows have been temporarily suspended.
Are all the restaurants open at the Park?
The following restaurants are currently open for dining: Entry Plaza Kiosk | Mask must be worn Mamma Rossella | Mask must be worn Espresso Rosso| Mask must be worn Il Podio | Open | Mask must be worn The following dining outlets remain temporarily closed: Ristorante Cavallino Officer’s Foodquarters Waffles & Crepes Pergola Kiosks
Do I need to wear a face mask on the rides?
For your own safety and wellbeing, you must wear a face mask at all times on all attractions. please note the following: Reusable or disposable (Single use surgical) masks are permitted on all attractions, except those mentioned below Reusable masks with an 'ear saver clip' must be worn on - Formula Rossa, Fiorano GT, Flying.
Are all the shops open at the Park?
urrently, only the Ferrari store is open. The Past & Present Store remains temporarily closed.
What precautionary measures are taken to protect guests from Coronavirus?
The safety and wellbeing of our guests is our top priority! Our preventive measures across the Park include: Extensive Cleaning and Sanitizing Protocol: Increased frequency of sanitizing and disinfection across the entire Park Reduced Capacity: Free of charge hand sanitizers have been placed across the Park for guests to use regularly Face Masks
